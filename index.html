About Six Feet Apart

Six Feet Apart was created during Florida International University's (FIU) PantherHacks 2020 hackathon. The problem statement of the hackathon was to develop a solution to a problem that has emerged due to the current COVID-19 pandemic. With that goal in mind, Six Feet Apart aims to connect people virtually as physical contact is extemely limited due to social distancing guidelines.
